CoconutGuard AI

Multimodal AI for Early Coconut Disease Detection

Version 2.1.0

Project Overview

CoconutGuard AI is an advanced agricultural technology solution that uses multimodal image fusion and hybrid deep learning to detect coconut tree diseases early. By combining RGB, Thermal, and NDVI imaging with state-of-the-art CNN architecture, we help farmers identify diseases like Bud Rot, Leaf Blight, and Yellowing Disease with over 91% accuracy, enabling timely intervention and crop protection.
